Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the use of the Anganwadi Monthly Inventory Report Web App (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We are highly committed to protecting the privacy and security of Anganwadi workers who use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal or sensitive user data (such as Aadhaar card numbers, bank details, phone numbers, or individual health records of citizens/children). We only process data directly related to managing your monthly stock:

  • Inventory Data: Monthly consumption logs, item descriptions, and stock quantities required to generate the administrative inventory sheets.

2. Data Storage and Hosting

All stock and inventory data entered by the user is securely stored on secure cloud servers via Google Cloud / Google Drive.

This data is used solely to generate report summaries and to allow users to view their historical records upon logging in.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard practices and cloud security measures to prevent unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure of your stock records.

We strictly do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third-party companies, marketing agencies, or external organizations.

4. Government Affiliation Disclaimer

This is an independent tool developed to simplify daily administrative and stock-keeping tasks for Anganwadi workers.

The App is not an official application of any government department, nor does it represent any government entity.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as new features are added to the App. Any updates will be reflected directly on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
